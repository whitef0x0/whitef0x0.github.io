nwHacks Micro is the first big hacker event of the year. We're putting together an 10 hour event for everyone to get a taste of what the full nwHacks experience will be like. Come out, make friends, and hack together something extraordinary.
APPLICATIONS CLOSE SEPTEMBER 25th.

Rules & Overview


Each team will have 30 minutes to setup and show their demo in ICCSX250.

A panel of judges will score the hacks based on creativity, UX, innovation and societal impact.

All hacks must be started on from scratch at the event. External libraries are allowed to be used but original code must be used for the actual hack. Failure to do so will result in immediate expulsion.

All hackers must abide by the code of conduct, found here . Any hacker found violating this code of conduct will be disqualified and asked to leave the premises immediately.

Entry cost for non-members is 10$, for StartupStorm members 5$

Teams must be 1-4 members maximum to compete

All Hacks must be submitted to the ChallengePost by 8pm for their hack to be considered for judging purposes. Late submissions will not be considered.
